The Best Movies According to Angela

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Oppenheimer (2023)

A gripping, intellectual biopic that explores the burden of genius and the consequences of power.

Ted Lasso

Feel-good, heartwarming, and unexpectedly deep — Ted Lasso is more than just laughs; it’s a masterclass in kindness and leadership.

Drive

Super beautiful film. Really artistic.
